# Research: Complete Tax Filing Checklist for Poland (2023-2025)

## Context

A Polish tax resident (since ~2023) who is a software engineer needs to understand ALL tax forms and filings required for tax years 2023, 2024, and 2025. Their income/activity sources:

### 2023
- **Crypto disposals**: Sold crypto for EUR on Binance and Kraken (taxable under PIT-38)
- **Possible personal income**: Need to determine if any service income was earned this year and how it was reported
- **No JDG**: Did not have a Polish company in 2023

### 2024
- **Crypto disposals**: Sold crypto for EUR on Binance and Kraken (taxable under PIT-38)
- **Possible personal/business income**: Need to determine
- **JDG status unclear**: May or may not have had JDG in 2024

### 2025
- **Crypto disposals**: Sold crypto (USDC, USDT, DOT) for EUR (taxable under PIT-38)
- **USDC salary income**: Received 40,500 USDC as payment for services (Jan-Apr 2025, before JDG)
- **JDG income**: After establishing JDG, invoiced clients in EUR, paid 12% ryczalt monthly
- **Crypto investments**: Resulting in net loss for the year

## Questions

### Which Forms Must Be Filed?
1. List ALL tax forms that must be filed for each year (2023, 2024, 2025):
   - PIT-36 (general income)?
   - PIT-36L (flat tax business income)?
   - PIT-37 (employment income)?
   - PIT-38 (capital gains including crypto)?
   - PIT-28 (ryczalt)?
   - PIT/ZG (foreign income annex)?
   - Any others?

2. For 2025 specifically, given the mix of:
   - Pre-JDG USDC salary income
   - JDG ryczalt income
   - Crypto capital gains/losses
   Which forms are needed and how do they interact?

3. Must PIT-38 be filed even if there is a net loss (no tax due)?

4. If the only income in 2023 was crypto gains, is PIT-38 the only form needed? Or must PIT-36/37 also be filed?

### VAT Considerations
5. Are there any VAT obligations for:
   - Crypto trading (selling crypto for fiat)?
   - Receiving USDC as payment for services?
   - JDG IT services to foreign clients?
6. Is crypto trading VAT-exempt in Poland (following EU CJEU ruling)?

### Social Insurance (ZUS)
7. Are there ZUS obligations from the USDC salary income received before JDG?
8. If the person had no formal employment or business registration in Jan-Apr 2025, what ZUS obligations apply to the USDC income?
9. Once JDG is established, does ZUS need to be paid from the start date or retroactively?

### Health Insurance (NFZ)
10. How does health insurance work for someone with ryczalt JDG?
11. Is the health insurance contribution (skladka zdrowotna) deductible from tax?
12. What happens for the months before JDG was established?

### Deadlines Summary
13. What are ALL relevant deadlines for 2023, 2024, and 2025 tax filings?
14. For late filings (2023, 2024), what deadlines still apply?
15. For 2025 (current year), what are the upcoming deadlines?

### International Obligations
16. Are there any reporting obligations related to foreign bank accounts, foreign exchanges, or foreign crypto wallets?
17. Must foreign income be reported on PIT/ZG annex?
18. Are there any CRS/FATCA implications for holding crypto on foreign exchanges?

## Desired Output

A comprehensive checklist table showing:
- Each form required per year
- Filing deadline (original and late-filing rules)
- What income/activity it covers
- Status (filed / needs filing / needs correction)
- Dependencies between forms
