# Research: General Polish Crypto Tax Framework Overview

## Context

This is a broad overview research document to build foundational understanding of how cryptocurrency is taxed in Poland. It should serve as the baseline knowledge document that the other specific research topics build upon.

## Questions

### Legal Foundation
1. What law governs crypto taxation in Poland? Is it primarily:
   - Ustawa o podatku dochodowym od osob fizycznych (PIT Act)?
   - If so, which specific articles (Art. 17, Art. 22, Art. 30b)?
   - Any separate crypto-specific legislation?

2. When did the current crypto tax regime take effect? (Reportedly January 1, 2019). What changed from the previous regime?

3. How does Polish law define "waluta wirtualna" (virtual currency)? Reference to Ustawa o przeciwdzialaniu praniu pieniedzy (AML law), Art. 2 ust. 2 pkt 26.

4. Does the definition cover:
   - Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other "standard" cryptocurrencies?
   - NFTs?
   - DeFi LP tokens?
   - Wrapped tokens (wBTC, stETH)?
   - Governance tokens?

### Tax Rate and Reporting
5. Confirm: 19% flat rate on net crypto capital gains (przychod minus koszty uzyskania przychodu)?

6. Is crypto income ever taxed at progressive rates (12%/32%)? Under what circumstances?

7. How does crypto taxation interact with:
   - PIT-36 (general income)?
   - PIT-36L (flat tax business income)?
   - PIT-28 (ryczalt)?
   - PIT-38 (capital gains)?

8. Can a JDG (sole proprietorship) report crypto trading as business income? Would that change the tax rate or treatment?

### EU Regulatory Context
9. How does EU DAC8 directive (mandatory crypto reporting by exchanges) affect Polish taxpayers starting 2026?

10. Will Binance and Kraken report Polish users' transactions to Polish tax authorities?

11. How does MiCA regulation affect crypto tax in Poland?

12. Are there any upcoming legislative changes to Polish crypto tax law (2025-2026)?

### Common Pitfalls
13. What are the most common mistakes Polish crypto taxpayers make?

14. What triggers a tax audit for crypto? Are tax authorities actively monitoring exchanges?

15. Is there any amnesty or voluntary disclosure program specifically for crypto?

16. What is the statute of limitations for crypto tax obligations in Poland?

### Decentralized Finance (DeFi)
17. How are DeFi activities taxed in Poland?
   - Providing liquidity to AMMs (Uniswap, etc.)?
   - Yield farming / liquidity mining?
   - Borrowing against crypto collateral?
   - Losses from smart contract exploits or rug pulls?

18. Are there any KIS (Krajowa Informacja Skarbowa) interpretations specifically about DeFi?

### Mining and Staking
19. How is crypto mining income taxed?
20. How are staking rewards taxed? At receipt or at disposal?
21. If staking rewards are received as a different token (e.g., DOT.S vs DOT), does the unstaking/migration create a taxable event?

## Desired Output

A comprehensive overview document covering the entire Polish crypto tax landscape, suitable as a reference guide. Include:
- Legal framework with references
- Tax rates and forms
- Classification of all common crypto activities
- Timeline of regulatory changes (2019 reform, MiCA, DAC8)
