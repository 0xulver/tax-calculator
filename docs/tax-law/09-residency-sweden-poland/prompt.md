# Research: Tax Residency Transition from Sweden to Poland

## Context

The taxpayer:
- Was a Swedish tax resident from before 2020 through approximately mid-2023
- Became a Polish tax resident around 2023 (moved to Poland)
- Had cryptocurrency investments throughout this period (since 2020)
- Actively traded crypto, received staking rewards, and participated in DeFi while in both countries
- Filed Swedish crypto taxes (K4 form) for at least some of the Swedish years
- Did NOT file Polish crypto taxes (PIT-38) for 2023 or 2024

The key issue: crypto was acquired in Sweden, may be sold in Poland, and cost basis needs to be traced across the transition.

## Questions

### Determining Tax Residency
1. How is tax residency determined in Poland? What makes someone a Polish tax resident (Art. 3 ust. 1 ustawy o PIT)?
   - Centre of vital interests (centrum interesow zyciowych)?
   - Physical presence (183 days)?
   - Which takes precedence?

2. Is it possible to have been a tax resident of BOTH Sweden and Poland for part of 2023 (during the transition)?

3. The Sweden-Poland double taxation treaty (DTT): how does it resolve dual residency (tie-breaker rules, Art. 4 of the DTT)?

4. If the taxpayer moved to Poland in, say, March 2023: are they a Polish tax resident for the entire year 2023, or only from March onwards?

### Worldwide vs Territorial Taxation
5. Poland taxes residents on worldwide income. Does this mean ALL crypto trades from January 2023 onwards (even those done before the physical move) are taxable in Poland?

6. If the taxpayer was a Swedish resident for Jan-Mar 2023 and Polish for Apr-Dec 2023: which country taxes the crypto gains from Jan-Mar?

7. Under the DTT, capital gains from crypto: which article applies? Article 13 (Capital Gains)? Does the DTT even cover crypto gains?

### Cost Basis Across Border
8. If BTC was bought in 2021 (while in Sweden) for EUR 30,000, and sold in 2024 (while in Poland) for EUR 50,000: what is the cost basis in PLN for Polish PIT-38? Is it the EUR purchase price converted at the NBP rate from the original 2021 purchase date?

9. Are there any special rules for assets acquired before becoming a Polish tax resident? Any "step-up" or "step-down" in basis?

10. If the taxpayer already paid Swedish capital gains tax on crypto sold in 2020-2022 while in Sweden, does Poland have any claim on those same gains?

### Swedish Tax Credits
11. If crypto gains are taxable in both countries for the transition year (2023): can the taxpayer claim a foreign tax credit in Poland for Swedish tax paid? How?

12. Must PIT/ZG (foreign income annex) be filed for the transition year?

### Practical Implications
13. For our FIFO calculator: should pre-Poland lots (bought 2020-2022 in Sweden) use the original EUR purchase price converted at the then-current NBP EUR rate? Or should they use the market value on the date of becoming Polish resident?

14. If the taxpayer filed Swedish K4 for 2022 showing crypto losses in Sweden: can those losses be used in Poland?

15. What happens with crypto acquired via Swedish employer (if any) -- does this create any complications?

## Desired Output

Clear rules for:
- When Polish tax residency starts
- How to handle the transition year (2023)
- Cost basis calculation for pre-Poland crypto
- DTT application to crypto gains
- Whether Swedish tax losses can transfer to Poland
