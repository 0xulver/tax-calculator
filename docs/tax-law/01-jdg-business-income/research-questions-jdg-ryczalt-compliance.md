# Research: JDG Business Income Reporting in Poland

## Context

A software engineer living in Poland operates a JDG (Jednoosobowa Dzialalnosc Gospodarcza -- Polish sole proprietorship). The business provides software development services (PKD 62.01.Z or similar) and invoices clients in EUR. The JDG was established sometime in 2025 (not from the start of the year). The owner chose the 12% flat tax (ryczalt od przychodow ewidencjonowanych) and pays monthly.

The business receives EUR to a Polish business bank account (likely mBank or similar). There is one or more B2B clients paying in EUR.

## Questions

### Monthly Obligations
1. What exactly are the monthly obligations under ryczalt 12%? Is it 12% of gross revenue with no deduction for costs? Confirm the rate for IT services (PKD 62.01.Z).
2. How does EUR invoicing work for ryczalt? Must the EUR amount be converted to PLN for tax purposes? If so, using what exchange rate (NBP mid-rate from which date exactly)?
3. What date is used for the EUR->PLN conversion for revenue recognition -- invoice date, payment date, or something else?
4. When are monthly tax payments due? What form or system is used to report them (JPK_V7, e-Urzad Skarbowy)?
5. Is ZUS (social insurance) a separate payment? What are the ZUS obligations and amounts for the first 2 years (ulga na start, preferencyjne)?

### Yearly Filing
6. What is the yearly tax return form for ryczalt? Is it PIT-28? What is the filing deadline?
7. What needs to be reported on the yearly form? Just total annual revenue? Or monthly breakdown?
8. If the JDG was established mid-year (say March 2025), how does the yearly filing work for the partial year?
9. Are there any additional annual declarations or information returns needed (e.g., for foreign clients, intra-community services)?
10. Does VAT registration matter here? If registered as VAT-exempt (zwolniony) vs VAT-registered, what changes?

### EUR-Specific Issues
11. Are there foreign exchange (FX) gains/losses on EUR held in the business bank account? If so, how are they taxed and reported?
12. When EUR is converted to PLN in the bank account, is that a taxable FX event?
13. If the business receives EUR and holds it without converting, are there any year-end revaluation requirements?

### Integration with Personal Taxes
14. How does ryczalt income interact with other personal income (e.g., crypto capital gains on PIT-38)? Are they completely separate?
15. Can business losses (if any) offset crypto gains or vice versa?
16. Is health insurance (skladka zdrowotna) calculated differently under ryczalt? What is the 2025 rate?

## Desired Output

A clear, sourced explanation of the complete annual and monthly tax compliance cycle for a ryczalt JDG doing IT services in EUR. Include specific form names, deadlines, and legal references (Ustawa o zryczaltowanym podatku dochodowym). Distinguish clearly between what was true for tax year 2024 vs 2025 if rules changed.
