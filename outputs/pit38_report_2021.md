## Legal Basis

- **Method**: Annual cost pooling per Art. 22 ust. 14-16, Art. 30b ust. 1a-1b ustawy o PIT
- **No FIFO**: Polish law does not require FIFO matching for crypto (Art. 30b ust. 7-7a does not apply to Art. 30b ust. 1a)
- **Crypto-to-crypto swaps**: NOT taxable (Art. 17 ust. 1f excludes exchange between virtual currencies)
- **Cost carry-forward**: Unlimited duration, no 50% annual cap (Art. 22 ust. 15-16)
- **Rate**: NBP mid-rate from last business day before transaction (Art. 11a ust. 1-2)
- **Stablecoins (USDC/USDT)**: Treated as waluta wirtualna (KIS interpretation, confirmed post-MiCA)
